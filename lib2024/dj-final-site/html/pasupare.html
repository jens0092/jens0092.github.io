In-fiction, Pastel*Palettes are the only band to have been started as a professional group, an idol group whose gimmick was that they would appear with instruments on stage. While their agency's original intent was for them to fake playing, their first concert was a catastrophe due to this ruse being exposed, and the group went on to dedicate themselves to learning to perform for real. They perform upbeat idol pop, often with fairly active participation by the non-primary-vocalist members.

Pastel*Palettes do not perform as a live band, though the original voice actor for their frontwoman had previous experience as an idol and did several in-character live solo shows. She departed the franchise for health reasons in Fall 2022 and has yet to be replaced, so it is unclear what the future holds for the band.

Characters, Cast and Instruments

  • Vocals - Aya Maruyama (Ami Maeshima, until September 2022)
  • Guitar - Hina Hikawa (Ari Ozawa)
  • Bass - Chisato Shirasagi (Sumire Uesaka)
  • Drums - Maya Yamato (Ikumi Nakagami)
  • Piano - Eve Wakamiya (Sawako Hata)
