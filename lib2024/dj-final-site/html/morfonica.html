In-fiction, Morfonica are a group of first-years at an elite private academy. Many people at the school have accomplished extraordinary things, and scholarship student Mashiro Kurata is overwhelmed by her talented classmates. All members joined the band for different reasons, but for all of them it is a way of finding a place where they belong and feel they can thrive.

Morfonica are the most recent of the major live groups to have been formed, being established only a couple of years ago. Several members are professional musicians -- notably, drummer mika was the instructor for the drummers of both Poppin'Party and Roselia -- but Amane Shindou was actually discovered through an open audition, becoming at 16 years old the youngest actress to be cast in the franchise.

Characters, Cast and Instruments

  • Vocals - Mashiro Kurata (Amane Shindou)
  • Guitar - Toko Kirigaya (Hina Suguta)
  • Bass - Nanami Hiromachi (Yuka Nishio)
  • Drums - Tsukushi Futaba (mika)
  • Violin - Rui Yashio (Ayasa)
